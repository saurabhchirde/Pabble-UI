Typography

Use typography to present your design and content as clearly and efficiently as possible.

Headings

For headings you can use h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6 elements.

Paragraphs

For your paragraph text you can use 'p' elements along with any of the size 'p-sm', 'p-md', 'p-lg', 'p-xl' or 'p-xxl'.

Text Align

For aligning position of your any headings or paragraphs, you have to specify alignment class as 'text-left' or 'text-center' or 'text-right' in your code to align the text.
